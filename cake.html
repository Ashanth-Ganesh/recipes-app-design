Vanilla Sponge Cake

A light and fluffy classic perfect for birthdays or tea-time treats.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9-inch round pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy, then add eggs one at a time.
  3. Stir in vanilla, then mix flour and baking powder.
  4. Add milk gradually and beat until smooth.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until golden brown.

Nutritional Info

Calories: 250 | Protein: 5g | Carbs: 32g | Fat: 11g

Allergens

Contains eggs, milk, and wheat.

Baking Tips

  • Use ingredients at room temperature for even mixing.
  • Don’t overmix—stop when the batter looks smooth.
  • Always preheat the oven for consistent results.
