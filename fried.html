Fried Mozzarella Sticks

Crunchy on the outside, gooey on the inside—these mozzarella sticks are an irresistible snack.

Ingredients

  • 12 mozzarella sticks
  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Coat cheese sticks in flour, dip in egg, and roll in breadcrumbs.
  2. Freeze for 30 minutes to prevent cheese from melting too quickly.
  3. Heat oil to 350°F and fry sticks until golden brown.
  4. Drain excess oil and serve with marinara sauce.

Nutritional Info

Calories: 400 | Protein: 20g | Carbs: 30g | Fat: 25g

Allergens

Contains milk, eggs, and wheat.

Frying Tips

  • Maintain oil temperature for even frying.
  • Do not overcrowd the pan.
  • Drain on paper towels to keep food crispy.
